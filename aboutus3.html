Art Wall art museum

Our art gallery shows many arts from talented artists.

Since Myanmar culture is getting noticed in the world, 

Myanmar arts society is getting wider than before. Our 

museum is formed by 4 art galleries which collobrated 

with our museum and are in our art museum.


SMART ART GALLERY

Small art gallery has many types of unique arts from 

Myanmar artists. Smart art gallery is the best selling 

art gallery in Mandalay. Smart art gallery was formed 

since 2002. Our ART WALL and SMART art gallery has been 

collaborated since 2019 and we really appreciate to 

colloborate with them.



KHOON ART GALLERY

Khoon art gallery is the most famous art gallery in Mandalay
 
and unique artists are the artists of that art gallery. Khoon 

art gallery was formed since 2000 and we really appreciate to 

collobrate with them.
    


  
MINGALARBAR ART GALLERY

Mingalarbar art gallery has many historical arts from many 

world wide famous Myanmar artists. Mingalarbar art gallery 

is one of the historical art museums in Myanmar. Mingalarbar 

art gallery was formed since 1989 and we really appreciate to 

collobrate with them.



APRIL ART GALLERY

Gorgeous artists are from April art gallery.April art gallery

is the most popular art gallery among the teenagers. Gorgeous

art gallery was formed since 2008 and we really appreiate to 

collobrate with them.
